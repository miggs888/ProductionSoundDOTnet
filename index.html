Michael Solomon is a New York City Production Sound Mixer. With over years experience, he has worked in all genres including reality, documentary, commercial, corporate, narrative, and even VR. In addition to recording the highest-quality audio, he brings positive energy to set, remains calm in difficult situations, helps out fellow crew members, and is able to quickly adapt when things change (as they often do).
