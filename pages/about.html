ABOUT

Michael Solomon has been a freelance Sound Recordist in New York City since 2008. He is experienced in all genres including reality, documentary, commercial, corporate, and even VR. In addition to recording the highest-quality audio, he brings positive energy to set, remains calm in difficult situations, helps out fellow crew members, and is able to adapt when things change (as they often do).

He has worked with Michelle Obama, Bill Clinton, Oprah Winfrey, Keith Richards, George Clooney, Alex Rodriguez, Cyndi Lauper, Jack Black, Anthony Bourdain, and Bobby Flay, among others.
